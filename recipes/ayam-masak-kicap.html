Ayam Masak Kicap

Description

A traditional Malay cuisine, chicken cooked with sweet soy sauce, to be enjoyed daily and festivities especially during Eid.
The dish is flexible for its ease to prepare and enjoyed in variety flavour.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Shallot
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Holland Onion
  • Chilly
  • Tomato
  • Potato
  • Scallions
  • Sweet Soy Sauce
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Salt
  • White Pepper Powder
  • Water
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Wash all the chicken and vegetables.
  2. Remove all the skin of the potatoes, shallot, garlic and ginger.
  3. Cut the potatoes into wedges and soak it in water to keep it fresh.
  4. Cut the shallot, garlic and ginger into small pieces.
  5. Cut the holland onion into ring shape.
  6. Cut the chillies against its length so it becomes ring for each piece.
  7. Cut the tomatoes into wedges.
  8. Cut the scallions into small pieces.
  9. Crush the shallot, garlic and ginger using the mortar stone.
  10. Cut the chicken into small pieces and mixed it with turmeric powder and salt.
  11. Heat the oil.
  12. Fry the chicken until half cooked.
  13. Fry the potato wedged.
  14. Remove 3/4 of the oil.
  15. Fry the mixed crushed shallot, garlic and ginger until golden yellow.
  16. Add the chicken, sweet soy sauce, osyter sauce and water.
  17. Let it cook until the chicken is fully cooked.
  18. Add the chillies, potato wedges, tomato, white pepper powder and holland onion.
  19. Add water if the gravy is too little.
  20. Serve the dish into a plate.
  21. Sow some scallions on top of the dish.
  22. Enjoy. And remember to cook some rice.
