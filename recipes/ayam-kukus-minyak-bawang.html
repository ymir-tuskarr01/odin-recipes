Ayam Kukus Minyak Bawang

Description

An everyday Chinese cuisine that's easy to prepare with mild taste and invigorating flavour.
The dish is famous for its ease to prepare and light taste.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Oil
  • Scallion
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Shallot

Steps

  1. Wash all chicken and vegetables.
  2. Remove all the skin of garlic, ginger and shallot.
  3. Cut the chicken into small pieces.
  4. Cut the scallion into shorter pieces.
  5. Cut the garlic, ginger and shallot into small pieces.
  6. Heat the oil.
  7. Fry the mixed garlic, ginger and shallot until golden brown.
  8. Take out the mixed garlic, ginger and shallot.
  9. Fry the scallion until golden brown.
  10. Take out the scallion.
  11. Let the oil cooled down.
  12. Put the chicken inside a bowl.
  13. Add in the scallion oil.
  14. Put the bowl inside a steamer.
  15. Steam the chicken until fully cooked.
  16. Take out from the steamer.
  17. Serve the dish.
  18. Enjoy. And remember too cook some rice.
